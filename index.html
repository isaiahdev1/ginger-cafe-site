Our Story

A Family's Passion
On Every Plate.

Ginger Cafe is a family-run restaurant built on a simple belief — that great food starts with great ingredients. We bring together the depth of Chinese culinary tradition and the vibrant flavors of Southeast Asia, using fresh, seasonal produce available right here in the Bay Area.

From hand-crafted dim sum to our creative house specialties, every dish is an expression of craft, care, and culture.
